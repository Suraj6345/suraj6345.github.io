Similarity

Q1.

State the Basic Proportionality Theorem (BPT).

  • Statement: If a line parallel to a side of a triangle intersects the other two sides in distinct points, then the line divides those sides in the same ratio.
  • If in $\triangle ABC$, $DE \parallel BC$, then $\frac{AD}{DB} = \frac{AE}{EC}$.
Q2.

Explain the AAA, SAS, and SSS tests of similarity.

  • AAA: All three corresponding angles are congruent.
  • SAS: Two corresponding sides are in proportion and the included angle is congruent.
  • SSS: All three corresponding sides are in the same proportion.
Q3.

Theorem of Areas of Similar Triangles.

When two triangles are similar, the ratio of their areas is equal to the ratio of the squares of their corresponding sides.

$\frac{A(\triangle 1)}{A(\triangle 2)} = \frac{(s_1)^2}{(s_2)^2}$
