Disaster Management

Q1.

What are the objectives of Disaster Management?

  • Saving human life during a disaster.
  • Supply of food, water, and medicines to victims.
  • Restoring normal conditions by rehabilitation.
  • Taking precautions to reduce the impact of future disasters.
Q2.

List basic First Aid measures.

  • Fracture: Keep the part immobilized using a splint.
  • Burns: Hold the area under cold water for at least 10 minutes.
  • Bleeding: Apply pressure with a clean cloth.
  • Sunstroke: Move the person to a cool place and give water.
