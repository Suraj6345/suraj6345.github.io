Circle

Q1.

State the Tangent Theorem.

A tangent at any point of a circle is perpendicular to the radius at the point of contact.

Q2.

Explain Inscribed Angle Theorem.

The measure of an inscribed angle is half of the measure of the arc intercepted by it.

Formula: $\angle BAC = \frac{1}{2} m(arc BC)$

Q3.

What is a Cyclic Quadrilateral?

If all vertices of a quadrilateral lie on the same circle, it is called a cyclic quadrilateral. The opposite angles of a cyclic quadrilateral are supplementary ($180^\circ$).
