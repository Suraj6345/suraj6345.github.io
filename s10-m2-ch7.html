Q1.

List important Volume formulas.

  • Cylinder: $\pi r^2 h$
  • Cone: $\frac{1}{3} \pi r^2 h$
  • Sphere: $\frac{4}{3} \pi r^3$
  • Hemisphere: $\frac{2}{3} \pi r^3$
Q2.

What is the Area of a Sector and Length of an Arc?

  • Area of Sector: $\frac{\theta}{360} \times \pi r^2$
  • Length of Arc ($l$): $\frac{\theta}{360} \times 2\pi r$
  • Relation: Area of sector $= \frac{l \times r}{2}$
