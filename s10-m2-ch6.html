Trigonometry

Q1.

State the Trigonometric Identities.

  • $\sin^2 \theta + \cos^2 \theta = 1$
  • $1 + \tan^2 \theta = \sec^2 \theta$
  • $1 + \cot^2 \theta = \text{cosec}^2 \theta$
Q2.

Angle of Elevation and Depression.

  • Elevation: Angle formed when looking up at an object.
  • Depression: Angle formed when looking down at an object.
  • These concepts are used to find heights and distances of towers, buildings, or ships.
